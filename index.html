Floor Plans & 3D Tours

Formats, delivery, and revisions

We offer three formats — all measured on-site during your photography appointment:

  • 2D Standard Floor Plans — detailed with room dimensions, labels, and fixtures. Most popular for MLS and rental listings.
  • 3D Rendered Floor Plans — photorealistic with furnishings, textures, and a lifelike perspective of the space.
  • Interactive Floor Plans — clickable, zoomable, and embeddable versions of any 2D plan, delivered with a shareable link and embed code.

Pricing varies by plan type and property size. See the pricing section on this page for current rates.

Floor plans are delivered within 24 hours of your photography appointment, alongside your edited photos. 3D rendered plans may take up to 24 hours due to additional processing. Interactive floor plans are delivered with a shareable link and embed code.

Commercial floor plans for complex multi-floor properties may require an additional business day. Rush delivery is available — contact us to discuss your timeline.

We offer free revisions within 24 hours of delivery. If a label, dimension, or layout detail needs correction, we fix it at no charge. Our digital measurement process is highly accurate, but we want every floor plan to meet your standards before it goes live.

Most multi-family clients stage 2–3 key rooms per floor plan — living area, primary bedroom, and one flex space. When staging multiple floor plans, we match style and furnishings across variants so your gallery reads as a cohesive lease-up campaign rather than isolated one-offs.

Virtual staging is delivered within 24–48 hours after you select your photos and design style. Turnaround and revisions are the same regardless of how many plans we stage.
